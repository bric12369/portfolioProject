About Me:

Hi, my name is Barry and I'm an aspiring web developer with a passion for creating intuitive and visually appealing websites. For the past six months, I've been dedicated to learning web development, building a foundation in coding and design that I'm excited to expand further.

Transitioning into web design allows me to combine my creativity with technical problem-solving, crafting digital experiences that are both functional and engaging. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey so far, and I'm eager to continue growing and connecting with others who share my passion for design and development.
